Let's Talk About it

Let's Talk About it is a website that will offer a truly comprehensive and inclusive mental health education American public schools often fail to provide. This website will offer information on topics including DBT key concepts like validation, extreme emotions, acceptancr, positivity, coping mechanisms, problem solving, and mindfulness. This is meant to be another additioinal resource that teens and young adults can use to find accurate, current, and intersectional information on mental health.
