Extreme Emotions

TIPP skills for managing extreme emotions

T- temperature. Change the temperature of your face with cold water

I- intense exersize. To calm down your body when it is revved up

P- accepted breathing. Slow your pace of breathing way down

P- progressive muscle relaxation. Tense and relax each muscle group

vacation- unplug, take a walk, read a magazine, get your favorite drink, ect.

encouragment- cheerlead yourself. Repeat over & over "I can stand it"
