About the Team

MANDY WILLIAMS founder & developer

Amanda (Mandy) Williams came up with the idea of Let's Talk About it in February 2022. She wanted to provide inclusive and comprehensive menteal health information to bridge the gap in accurate information, resources, and menteal health positivity that high school education programs lack. In May 2022 she was thinking about website ideas for her Intro to Web Design final and thought that creating a website for the Let's Talk About it would be the perfect task.
